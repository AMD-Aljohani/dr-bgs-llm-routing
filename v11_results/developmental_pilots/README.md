# Developmental trace-risk diagnostics

These files record exploratory diagnostics run after the primary absolute-SLO
protocol had been locked. They were used to understand why the strict gate
abstained and to design the separately locked noninferiority follow-up.

They are **not** primary or confirmatory evidence, are not used for headline
numerical claims, and must not be interpreted as preregistered analyses.
The paper's reported trace results are generated from:

- `trace_risk_runs.csv` and `trace_risk_by_scenario.csv`;
- `trace_noninferiority_runs.csv` and `trace_noninferiority_by_scenario.csv`;
- the corresponding protocol files and SHA-256 lock manifests.
